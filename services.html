Our Services

Bethany Home Care provides an array of home health services focused on helping patients become as independent as possible. We coordinate closely with patients, physicians, and families to develop individualized care plans.

We offer specialized disease management programs designed to reduce re-hospitalization rates and promote long-term wellness.

Specialized Programs

  • Urinary Tract Infection (UTI) Prevention Program
  • IV Therapy including Antibiotic and Hydration Therapy
  • Respiratory Management (CHF & COPD)
  • Diabetes Care Management
  • Cardiac Care Management
  • Physical Therapy with Fall Prevention Program

Bethany Home Care has proudly served Riverside County since 1995. We are a California State Licensed, Joint Commission Accredited, and Medicare Certified home health agency.

Our proven programs include fall prevention, wound care management, UTI prevention, and Congestive Heart Failure (CHF) management — all designed to promote independence and quality of life.
